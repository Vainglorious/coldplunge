Primary page structure

Use Frost backgrounds with white product cards. Reserve Alpine Navy for hero areas, footer sections and strong contrast moments.

Accent discipline

Glacier Blue drives calls to action. Cedar supports craftsmanship. Maple Red appears only in small Canadian details, badges or focus moments.

Shape language

Use subtle 8-16px corner radii, thin Stainless borders and generous whitespace. Avoid overly rounded, playful or neon styling.
