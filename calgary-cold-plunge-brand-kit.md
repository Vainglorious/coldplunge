# Calgary Cold Plunge - Website Brand Kit

## Fonts

- **Display and headings:** Inter Display ExtraBold/Bold. Use uppercase for hero headings and major product statements with `0.035em` to `0.055em` letter spacing.
- **Body and interface:** Inter Regular, Medium and SemiBold.
- Keep the company logo as artwork; do not recreate its lettering with a substitute font.

## Core colours

| Role | Name | HEX |
|---|---|---|
| Primary dark | Alpine Navy | `#1B2C38` |
| Primary action | Glacier Blue | `#2C6D9E` |
| Supporting blue | Ice Blue | `#5E91B5` |
| Warm material accent | Cedar | `#9D5B2A` |
| Deep wood accent | Deep Cedar | `#6F3015` |
| Canadian accent | Maple Red | `#751516` |
| Metal/border neutral | Stainless | `#ABB4BA` |
| Page background | Frost | `#F7F9FA` |
| Cards/negative space | White | `#FFFFFF` |

## Recommended balance

Use approximately 55% Frost/White, 25% Alpine Navy, 12% Glacier/Ice Blue, 6% cedar tones and no more than 2% Maple Red.

## Accessibility

- Alpine Navy on white has excellent contrast.
- Glacier Blue can be used for normal-size white button text.
- Ice Blue should be reserved for large headings, icons and decorative accents on light backgrounds.
- Stainless is for borders and surfaces, not body text.

## Website direction

Use Frost backgrounds, white cards, Alpine Navy hero/footer sections, Glacier Blue calls to action and cedar accents around craftsmanship or product materials. Keep corners moderately rounded and layouts spacious and premium.
